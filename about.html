Skee Mask

ABOUT

Skee Mask is Bryan Müller (a.k.a. SCNTST), a versatile DJ/producer from Munich, Germany. Born in 1993 (age 28).

Skee Mask’s direction is mostly rolling rhythms, combined with electronic soundscapes that result in a deep atmospheric vibe. He has been influenced by a lot of different types of music in the past, which made his productions pretty varied, but his biggest influences are “classic” techno labels like Chain Reaction, Warp Records or Counterbalance. Skee Mask released its debut album "Shred" in 2016.
